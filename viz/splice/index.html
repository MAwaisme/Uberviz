Splice Visualizer

A realtime interactive music visualizer, using time-code data from Splice's upcoming visualizer API. This data enables tightly synced visuals that perfectly align to the beats of the song.

Use the mouse to move the camera and use the audio controls to skip around the song. Visuals are randomized to give a different experience with each viewing.

Music is Henry Fong & J-Trick - Scream (Original Mix).

Built with Three.js, GSAP, WebGL and Web Audio API.
